Learning Roadmaps

Follow our structured paths to go from beginner to electronics expert

Beginner Level (0-3 months)

Build your foundation with these essential skills

  • Basic electronic components and circuits
  • Introduction to Arduino programming
  • Simple sensor interfacing
  • Basic prototyping techniques
  • 5 beginner projects completed

Intermediate Level (3-6 months)

Develop more advanced electronics skills

  • Advanced microcontroller features
  • Wireless communication (Bluetooth/WiFi)
  • PCB design basics
  • Data logging and visualization
  • 3 intermediate projects completed

Advanced Level (6-12 months)

Master professional-level electronics

  • RTOS and multitasking
  • IoT system design
  • Signal processing
  • Custom PCB fabrication
  • 2 advanced projects completed

Professional Level (12+ months)

Specialize and build your career

  • Industrial automation
  • Robotics and control systems
  • AI at the edge
  • Product development lifecycle
  • Portfolio of professional projects
